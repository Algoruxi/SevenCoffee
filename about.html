About Us

Serving Since 1950

Our Story

Seven Coffee was founded by Group 7, who were inspired by their lecturer diverse and complex world of coffee.

We source only the finest beans from around the world and use traditional roasting methods to create a sensory experience in every cup. We are committed to sustainability and ethical practices, ensuring fair pay for farmers and environmentally-friendly harvesting. At Seven Coffee, we aim to create a community of coffee lovers and invite you to join us in discovering the wonders of coffee.

Our Vision

At Seven Coffee, our vision is to bring the world of specialty coffee to everyone. We believe that coffee should be an enjoyable and approachable experience for everyone, from casual drinkers to connoisseurs. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical practices is at the heart of everything we do. We strive to build a community of coffee lovers who share our passion and values. Our goal is to make Seven Coffee a trusted and respected name in the coffee industry, known for our exceptional coffee and our dedication to creating a better world for coffee farmers and their communities.
